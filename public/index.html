join.report
a csv soundsystem, mhkeller project

Welcome to join.report

Drag two datasets to start. The left dataset will have matching values from the right dataset joined to it based on the column you choose as the common key.

You can upload a csv, tsv, psv (pipe-separated) or dbf file. Try an example.

Data too big? Join on the command line or in NodeJS.
